Tips

  • The tofu is really soft... so cut it however you like.
  • Don't stir too hard and break the tofu!!!
Ingredients
16 oz soft tofu
2 green onions
1 tbsp neutral cooking oil
3/4 teaspoon hon dashi
1 tbsp cornstarch
1/2 tbsp Shaoxing cooking wine
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Slice the green onion and separate the white and green parts
  2. Dice the tofu into 1/3 inch cubes
  3. Mix all sauce ingredients together in a cup or bowl.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon cooking oil in a medium pan at medium heat
  5. Add white portion of green onions to pan and saute for 30 seconds
  6. Add diced tofu to the pan and saute and stir gently until heated, about 1 minute
  7. Stir in well mixed sauce ingredients into pan
  8. Saute and stir gently until sauce thickens and coats all the tofu
  9. Turn off the heat and add stir in green part of green onions

Time to make this recipe

Prep time: 10-15 mins
Cook time: 5 minutes

Servings: 5
